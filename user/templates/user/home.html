How it Works


Report an Issue

Use the CitySight website or mobile app to report local issues such as potholes, broken streetlights, and graffiti.

Track Progress

Stay informed about what issues have been reported in your area and track the status of those issues.

Join the Conversation

Upvote or comment on reported issues to provide valuable insight and help local government prioritize which issues to address first.

Why Use CitySight


Easy and Accessible

The user-friendly and accessible platform makes it easy for anyone to report local issues without the need to navigate confusing government websites or make phone calls.

Transparency and Accountability

CitySight helps increase transparency and accountability between local government and the community.

Empowerment

Residents are empowered to take an active role in improving their community by using CitySight.

Community Engagement

CitySight promotes community engagement by allowing residents to upvote or comment on reported issues.
